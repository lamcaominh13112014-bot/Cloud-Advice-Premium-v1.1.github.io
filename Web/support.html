User Guide & Documentation

Mastering Cloud Advice Premium v1.1 step by step.

📥

Download & Launch

  • Purchase & Access: After the $500 payment, you will receive a secure link to download the Premium Package.
  • Extraction: Right-click the downloaded .zip file and select "Extract Here". You will see the LTP_Launcher file.
  • Initialization: Double-click the Launcher. The system will automatically check your infrastructure and prepare the 100TB connection.
📋

Interface Navigation

🗂️ Sidebar Control

The sidebar on the left is your command center. Use it to switch between Dashboard, Data Storage, and Security Settings instantly.

🔍 Advanced Search

Located at the top bar. Just type any keyword, and our engine will scan your 100TB infrastructure to find your files in milliseconds.

☁️ Upload Button

Look for the "Cloud" icon. Click it to drag and drop files directly into the decentralized network. Progress is shown in real-time.

🛡️

Security & Account

🔑 Password Creation

Go to Settings > Security. Set a strong password. Our system uses industry-standard encryption to protect this local key.

📱 OTP Verification

Every time you log in from a new location, a 6-digit OTP is required. Check your registered secure channel to receive the code.

🔄 Forgot Password

If you lose your key, click "Forgot Password". You must pass a 2-step OTP verification to reset your access credentials.

💾

Data Persistence (cloud_data.json)

The cloud_data.json file is the heart of your settings. Do not delete this file! It ensures that your sidebar customizations, search history, and infrastructure links are saved forever.
